about us

What we do & why we do?

We collect waste and recycle this waste into other usable things.Recycling is the process of collecting and processing materials that would otherwise be thrown away as trash and turning them into new products.Recycling often saves energy and natural resources.Natural resources include land, plants, minerals, and water. When we use materials more than once, we conserve natural resources.There are many reasons why recycling is essential. Not only can it help reduce your carbon footprint, but it also helps reduce the need for harvesting raw materials, saves energy, reduces greenhouse gases, prevents pollution, and more.By improving our recycling habits, we can help keep the environment clean and preserve our natural resources.
